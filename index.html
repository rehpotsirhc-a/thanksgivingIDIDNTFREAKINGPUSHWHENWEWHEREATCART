Modern Thanksgiving

Thanksgiving today is a holiday that is all about expressing gratitude for the things we all enjoy in life. For some it might be family and firends. For others it might be material things like a new car or home. And for some it might be smaller things like a new toy or video game. Whateber it is, there is always somehting to be thankful for in todays world. Sometimes it can be hard to find such a thing, but if you search for it long enough youll inevitably find it, no matter how hard times get you can always be thankful for someone or something in your life.
