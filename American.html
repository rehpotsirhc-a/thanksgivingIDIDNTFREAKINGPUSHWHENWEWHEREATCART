American Thanksgiving Traditions

The American Thanksgiving experiance is one of rememberance. Remembering the nations voyagers to the land we call home today is a vital part of the holiday. The pilgrims where those voyagers, seeking refuge from great britain in the lush and fruitful land of east coast of the americas. The region they landed on with the mayflower in the 1620s would be named plymouth. To survive, they famously made an alliance with the native americans in the aera, and had a feast in the name of that alliance they had a three day feast, where turkey was a popular item on the menu.

Celebration

The typical image of an american thanksgiving is people eating in comfort around a large table surrounded by family. the young and old converse with eachother about thier differences. lovers swooning over eachother. and the married reminicing the times they too had blossoming love. All while eating all american foods and delicacies.

The food

American thanksgiving dishes can be traced back centuries ago. Being immigrants from britain, they brought over plenty of enlgish crops to sustain themselves. turnips, cabbage, parsnips, onions, carrots, parsley, sage, rosemary, and thyme where all plants cultivated by the pilgrims to survive and where commonly eaten during the first thanksgiving and onward. Nowadays, more modern dishes are prefered. like pumpkin pie, stuffing, gravy mashed potatoes, cranberry sauce and corn.
