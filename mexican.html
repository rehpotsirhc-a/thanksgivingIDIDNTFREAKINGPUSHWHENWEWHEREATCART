Modern Thanksgiving

Thanksgiving today is a holiday that is all about expressing gratitude for the things we all enjoy in life. For some it might be family and friends. For others it might be material things like a new car or home. And for some it might be smaller things like a new toy or video game. Whatever it is, there is always something to be thankful for in today's world. Sometimes it can be hard to find such a thing, but if you search for it long enough you'll inevitably find it, no matter how hard times get you can always be thankful for someone or something in your life.

Celebration

The typical style of celebrating thanksgiving is with a large feast alongside family and friends around the table, taking turns saying thanks for all you have in life. After thanks, families often engage each other in conversation regarding a variety of topics. some more toxic than others (politics). Foods seen on the table include the obvious turkey, along with ham, gravy, corn, vegetables, cranberry and a slew of other foods that the family can indulge in on this special occasion.
